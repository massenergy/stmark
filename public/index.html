About Our School

St. mark Kiserian mixed Secondary School is a mixed day school located near Kiserain Town in Kajiado North constituency.
The school opened its doors to student in 1985 and was then just called Kiserian Mixed Secondary School.
It's name was changed to St. Mark Kiserian Mixed Secondary School in 2021.
